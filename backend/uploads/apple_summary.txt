Apple Inc. is an American multinational technology company headquartered in Cupertino, California. 
It designs, manufactures, and markets consumer electronics, computer software, and online services. 
Apple is best known for its iPhone, iPad, Mac computers, Apple Watch, and Apple TV. 
The company also develops operating systems like iOS, macOS, watchOS, and services such as the App Store, Apple Music, and iCloud. 
Founded in 1976 by Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak, and Ronald Wayne, Apple is one of the world's most valuable companies.
